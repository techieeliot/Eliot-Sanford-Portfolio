Things About Me

I'm currently seeking a full-time JavaScript role and provide consultation for small businesses as a React.js software engineering contractor.

I taught myself to code five years, and since then, I've always been learning on my own including developing automated tasks at work, creating personal projects, and organizing virtual and in-person study groups.
